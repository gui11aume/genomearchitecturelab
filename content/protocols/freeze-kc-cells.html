Kc cells freezing protocol

This protocol is used to freeze Drosophila Kc167 cells for long term storage.

Material:

  • BPYE medium containing 10% FBS
  • DMSO
  • Cryotubes
  • Ice-filled box

Procedure:

  1. Grow Kc cells to ∼ 2 million per mL.
  2. Transfer 10 mL Kc cells into a 15 mL Falcon tube.
  3. Centrifuge Falcon tubes at 1200 rpm for 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare cryotubes by adding 100μL DMSO.
  5. Resuspend the cell pellet in 1 mL BPYE medium containing 10% FBS.
  6. Transfer cells to cryotubes. Immediately put on ice.
  7. Leave cryotubes at -80°C for 2 days.
  8. Transfer cryotubes to liquid nitrogen tank.
