The Lab Notes

The Lab Notes are about what is going on in the team. They can be short technical notes, experimental tips, conceptual manifestos, mini tutorials etc.

The Lab Notes is a great way for the team members to practice their scientific writing skills, and a great way to share, because after all that’s what we like.

About the lab
